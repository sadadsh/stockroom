Color identity — pick the accent

The app is a professional KiCad library manager, today pure neutral grey. We're evolving the look. The one decision that sets the whole tone: keep it hueless, or introduce a single restrained accent that rides only on interactive chrome — the primary button, the selected nav item, the subtab underline, focus rings — and never on data.

Every tile below is the same Library panel, shown four ways. The pin-map/status colors (the green "Active" dot) stay exactly as they are regardless — those encode data, not brand.
